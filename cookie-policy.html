2. What cookies are

2.1

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, to keep them secure, and to help site operators understand how their services are used. Similar technologies, such as browser local storage and pixels, work in comparable ways; in this policy we refer to all of them as “cookies”.

4. Managing your cookies

4.1

You can also control cookies through your browser settings, including deleting cookies already stored and preventing new ones from being set. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may stop parts of the platform from working correctly.
