Sign your engagement letter

This confirms we’re acting for you. Please read it, then add your signature below.

Letter of Engagement

1. Our engagement

This letter sets out the basis on which Everest & Co Accountants Limited (trading as FileCGTOnline) will act for you in the preparation and submission of your Capital Gains Tax return to HM Revenue & Customs.

2. Your responsibilities

You are responsible for providing complete and accurate information relating to your disposal, including sale and purchase details, allowable costs, and any reliefs you may be entitled to. You remain legally responsible for the contents of your return, which we will prepare for your approval before submission.

3. Our responsibilities

We will calculate your Capital Gains Tax liability based on the information provided, prepare a draft return for your review, and submit it to HMRC once you have approved it. We will act with reasonable care and skill and in accordance with ICAEW professional standards.

4. Fees

Our fee for this service is fixed and payable in advance, as shown at checkout. Additional work outside the agreed scope will be quoted separately and only undertaken with your approval.

5. Data protection

We process your personal data in line with UK GDPR and our Privacy Policy. Your documents are stored securely and shared only with those working on your case.

6. Accuracy guarantee

Where we make an error in preparing your return, we will correct it at no cost to you and support you in any related correspondence with HMRC.

— End of Letter of Engagement —
